    Our Mission

    During the peak hours in usual business days, a long waiting line can be so much troublesome to the consumers. Waiting is not only irritating, but can be very tiring to both public consumers and staffs.Sometimes due to the long queue, disorder usually occurs.Our mission is Queue management systems are making to enhance the productivity and helping to reduce the time of waiting.

    Our Plan

    The Automated Queue Management System is design to manage certain customers with single department. This queuing architecture model can switch between different scheduling algorithms according to the testing result. Example the average waiting time by using two different queue control systems, which have developed status and take decision which customer to be served first.
